Electa Inauguration

About

On 23rd August 2024, the "ELECTA" association's inauguration and technical talk were held. The event began with a prayer to set a solemn tone for the proceedings. Mr. Abhinad A S, President of "ELECTA," delivered the welcome address, outlining the association's vision and goals for the year ahead. Mr.Brigadier Shaji, the Accademic Director of the college, followed with his Presidential address, emphasizing the importance of student associations for personal and professional growth. Dr. Jayaranjini N, Head of the Electronics and Communication Engineering Department, provided the inaugural address, highlighting the role of such associations in bridging academic knowledge with practical applications. The felicitation segment honored key individuals, including Ms.Anjana N from the ECE faculty,Ms. Mubeena, Joint Secretary of "ELECTA," and Mr. Sonu S, Vice President of "ELECTA," for their contributions.Mr.Nijin S S, Secretary of the association, delivered the vote of thanks, expressing gratitude to all attendees and contributors. The event concluded with a technical talk by Mr. Gokul,CEO of Bit Builders who presented on contemporary topics in electronics and communication engineering, offering valuable insights and enhancing the attendees' knowledge. Overall, the inauguration and technical talk were successfully conducted, marking a promising start for "ELECTA" and encouraging active student involvement in its future activities.
