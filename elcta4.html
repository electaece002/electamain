PCB Fabricaton and Designing

PCB design turns ideas into circuits

About

On 16th November 2024, the “ELECTA” Electronics and Communication Technical Association conducted a Hands-on PCB Design and Fabrication program for first-year ECE students. The aim of the program was to introduce students to basic electronic circuits, components, and tools. The program was conducted by Mr. Nijin S. S., Mr. Aswin Biju, Mr. Vaishnav H., and Mr. Abhinand A. S., final-year ECE students. The participants designed an Astable Multivibrator using PCB. The introduction to the circuit was given by Mr. Nijin S. S., followed by a designing session conducted by Mr. Abhinand A. S.. Then, an introduction to the PCB design software was delivered by Mr. Aswin Biju. Later, the PCB fabrication was carried out, and the output was successfully obtained.
