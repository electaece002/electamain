About

ELECTA, the Electronics and Communication Technical Association, is a vibrant community of innovators and enthusiasts dedicated to exploring, developing, and sharing knowledge in the realms of electronics and communication engineering.

ELECTA Inauguration

We are thrilled to announce the Inaugural Ceremony of ELECTA 2K24 on August 23, which was followed by a technical talk on embedded systems by Bit Builders. The event began with a prayer to set a solemn tone for the proceedings. Abhinand A S, President of ELECTA, delivered the welcome address, outlining the association's vision and goals for the year ahead. Mr. Shaji, the Academic Director of the college, followed with his presidential address, emphasizing the importance of student associations for personal and professional growth.

PCB Fabrication and Designing

ELECTA invites 1st-year ECE students to a 1-day hands-on training session on PCB design and fabrication on November 16, 2024. The students of the first year got a platform to sharpen their skills. The aim of the program was to introduce the students to basic electronic circuit components and tools. The program was conducted by Abhinand A S, Nijin S S, Aswin Biju, and Vaishnav H. ELEXA

ELEXA 2K25 is the premier tech fest organized by the Electronics and Communication Engineering Department, showcasing cutting-edge innovations and exciting competitions. The events are planned in such a way that students from different colleges can compete and participate, showcasing their talents and gaining experience. ELEXA, in association with VEGA 2K25, is happening on February 20. Join us and be a part of this electrifying journey into the future of technology.
